About Us

Welcome to University Dental Clinic, located in the heart of Riverside! Our clinic is dedicated to providing high-quality, comprehensive dental care to the local community. At University Dental Clinic, we offer a wide range of dental services to meet your needs, including routine cleanings, fillings, extractions, root canals, and more. Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with the latest dental technology, ensuring that you receive the most advanced and effective treatments available. In addition to our commitment to patient care, we also believe in education and prevention. We work closely with our patients to provide them with the knowledge and resources they need to maintain optimal oral health between visits. Whether you're a long-time patient or new to our clinic, we welcome you to our community of dental health. Thank you for choosing University Dental Clinic for your dental needs.
