About Us

Our team of dental professionals includes experienced dentists, hygienists, and support staff who are committed to providing exceptional patient care. We take the time to listen to our patients, understand their needs and concerns, and develop personalized treatment plans to help them achieve their dental goals. At University Dental Clinic, we believe that education and prevention are key to maintaining good oral health. That's why we are dedicated to educating our patients on the importance of good oral hygiene, proper nutrition, and routine dental care. We are proud to serve the Riverside community and look forward to helping our patients achieve healthy, beautiful smiles that last a lifetime. Contact us today to schedule your appointment and experience the University Dental Clinic difference.
